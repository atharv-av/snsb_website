    Requirement to open Current Bank Account:

  • Requirement to open Current Bank Account:
  • Two photographs, ID of proprietor/Partners/Directors/Authorised persons.
  • Address proof of Propritors/Partners/Directors/Authorised/persons
  • Copy of PAN CARD of firm, company, trust, society and individual.
  • Declaration regarding credit facility availed, if any, from another Bank.

  • Copy of registration certificate/Licenses issued by any two concerned Govt. deptt. (For example- Vat license, Durg license/Food license/Shop & co,,. Estt. Registration Certificate. Certificate of registration with Nager Nigam/K.U.M. Samiti), etc.

    • In case of public or private ltd. corporation

    • Copy of Certification of incorporation.
    • Copy of certificate of commencement of Business ( for public limited companies only)
    • Copy of Memorandum of Association and Articles of Association.
    • Certified copy of resolution passed by BOD regarding opening of account with UMUCB and authorizing the signatories to operate the said.
