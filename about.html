About Bank

The bank was registered in Madhya Pradesh Sahkarita Samiti ACT 1960 under section 9 on 6th July 1995. After completing all the legal formalities the Reserve Bank Of India issued Banking licence on 01 January 1996 and bank has commenced its banking activities w.e.f. 20.11.1996 at Mohan Bhai Complex, New road, Shajapur with humble means, has not looked back and is making growth as each year passes due to its professional management and dynamic leadership of Mr Manak Chand Bothra Chairman and team in Last 27 years.
